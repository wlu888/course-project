Eastmont Community Center

Food Distribution Program

Food distributions are by Appointments Only and Community members pick up food In-Person -
Serving all families in the community who are registered through Eastmont's Emergency Food Assistance program.

Clients are provided with 60-80 pounds of food each week every Friday from 8 - 10 am.

Clients are provided with shelf stable items, meat, diary, fresh fruits and vegetables.

Additional programs and resources are also provided.
